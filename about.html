Our Legacy

Three generations of spice expertise from the heart of Kerala

A Heritage of Excellence

Since 1962, AL-ATHEEM has been synonymous with premium quality spices. What started as a small family business in the spice gardens of Idukki has grown into a trusted name in the global spice trade.

Our founder, Mohammed Al-Atheem, began this journey with a simple vision: to bring the finest spices from Kerala's pristine hills to kitchens around the world. His deep understanding of spice cultivation and processing, passed down through generations, forms the foundation of our quality standards today.

We work directly with over 200 small-scale farmers across Kerala, ensuring fair prices and sustainable farming practices. Our commitment extends beyond business ‚Äì we're part of a community that has been nurturing these precious spices for centuries.
